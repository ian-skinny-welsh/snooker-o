Rules for the Open class

This is essentially a normal Score event:

  1. Collect all the controls in any order you like - do not punch any control more than once as that will cost you penalty points.
  2. The time limit is 60 minutes with punching start and punching finish. Maximum points gained in the minimum time wins.
  3. The basic 'punch' score is adjusted by mispunch and time penalties and then by the BOF recommended age/sex handicap values to get an overall score.
  4. Lateness penalties of 20 points per minute and part minute will be deducted if you return after the maximum allowed time.
  5. As the event is loosely based on the scoring format of snooker. There are 16 ‘balls’, 10 reds and six different colours. Red balls are worth 10 points, yellow 20, green 30, brown 40, blue 50, pink 60 and black 70. Controls representing the balls are pre-marked on the map in the correct colour, control code and the nominal points value.
