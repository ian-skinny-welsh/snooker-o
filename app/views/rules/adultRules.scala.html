Clearing the Reds

  1. The reds must be cleared before attempting the colours. However every time a red is cleared, a colour of your choice must be punched next. Treat each red as part of a Red-Colour pair until all the reds have been cleared.
  2. The choice of which colour ball you punch after each red is up to you - however this is Orienteering. Once you have been to a colour the first time you will then know where it is located. So while clearing the reds each subsequent visit to that colour will score 25% fewer points. For instance, the first time you visit the PINK it will be worth 60 points, but 45 the second time, 30 the third time, 15 the fourth time – and 0 for any other visits.
  3. Once all the Reds-Colour pairs are cleared; all the colours revert to their full values ready for clearing the colours.
  4. If you have not collected all 10 Red-Colour pairs, then any colours you collect will not score points; even if in the correct order.
