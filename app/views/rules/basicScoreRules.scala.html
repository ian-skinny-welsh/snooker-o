Basic rules

  1. This is a score event with time a limit of 60 minutes with a punching start and punching finish.
  2. The winner is the competitor with maximum points gained in the minimum time.
  3. The basic 'punch' score is adjusted by mispunch and time penalties and then by the BOF recommended age/sex handicap values to get an overall score.
    • Lateness penalties of 20 points per minute and part minute will be deducted if you return after the maximum allowed time.
    • A mispunched control/ball will cost you 40 points or the value of the 'ball'; whichever is the higher cost.
    • A 'Red' is mispunched if it is punched more than once, punched when a colour is required, or if it is punched while clearing the colours.
    • A 'Colour' is mispunched if it is punched when a Red is required or is punched out of sequence when clearing the colours.
  4. As the event is loosely based on the scoring format of snooker. There are 16 ‘balls’, 10 reds and six different colours. Red balls are worth 10 points, yellow 20, green 30, brown 40, blue 50, pink 60 and black 70. Controls representing the balls are pre-marked on the map in the correct colour, control code and the nominal points value.
